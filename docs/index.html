How it works

So you always know what to expect

01

Let’s connect

Everything starts with an open discussion, no sales pitch, no pressure. We take the time to understand your context, priorities, projects and the challenges you may have. The goal: make sure there is a real mutual fit and that we can bring concrete value before moving forward together.

02

Understand your needs

We analyze your needs in depth to define the scope, expectations and ideal consultant profile. We look beyond job titles and keywords to focus on real objectives, company culture, and the human fit that will make the collaboration successful.

03

Perfect match

Within 48 hours, you receive a shortlist of carefully selected freelance profiles that match both the hard skills required and the personality needed for your team. We coordinate interviews, provide insights to support your decision, and handle all administrative aspects so you can focus on choosing the right person.

04

Partnership & follow-up

Our role doesn’t stop once the consultant starts. We stay involved throughout the mission with regular check-ins, adjustments if needed, and support to ensure measurable impact and a smooth collaboration. We believe in long-term partnerships, not one-shot placements.

About

After graduating from Solvay, I started my career in audit, where I learned to dive into numbers, work under pressure, and communicate clearly with all kinds of stakeholders. That experience gave me a solid understanding of how companies really operate, and how smart financial insight can drive real decisions.

In 2021, I decided to combine this financial expertise with a more hands-on, commercial approach in consulting. I took on responsibilities as a business manager, learning how to manage projects, build strong client relationships, coordinate teams, and solve complex problems, all while keeping people at the center of everything.

Today, with Bridgi, our mission is clear: put people back at the center of staffing. we aim to eliminate unnecessary complexity, inflated fees, and transactional processes. We work as a trusted partner for companies, providing long-term support and guidance. Every aspect is transparent, from pricing to candidate selection. Profiles are carefully screened and assessed not just for skills, but for personality and cultural fit, with continuous follow-up to ensure success.

FAQ

What profiles do you specialize in?

We primarily focus on finance professionals, covering a wide range of roles. This includes positions such as accountant, controller, FP&A, finance manager, CFO, etc We also support other business functions, such as IT, procurement, HR, depending on client needs, always handpicking profiles that match the specific requirements and level of expertise you’re looking for.

Can you handle niche or highly specialized roles?

Absolutely, we leverage our extensive network of experienced professionals to identify candidates with the precise skills and expertise needed for specialized or hard-to-fill roles.

Do you work with startups or only established companies?

We work with both. From fast-growing startups to large organizations, we tailor our approach to your size, culture, and specific needs.

How do you evaluate soft skills?

We assess soft skills through in-depth conversations, reference checks, and situational discussions, ensuring candidates not only have the right mindset but also fit your team’s culture.
