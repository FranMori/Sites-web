FAQ

Questions? We’ve got you.

How fast can you deliver a freelancer?

In most cases, you receive a selection of curated profiles within 48 hours after our first in-depth discussion.

What profiles do you specialize in?

We focus on finance, operations and project roles where freelance expertise can unlock fast, tangible impact.

How do you ensure the right match between a freelancer and a company?

Beyond skills, we look at mindset, communication style and cultural fit. Every profile is pre-qualified via interviews, references when possible, and a deep understanding of your context.

What are your pricing models?

Our pricing is transparent and tailored to the assignment. We’re happy to walk you through options based on your needs and budget during a first call.

What makes your selection process different?

We don’t “CV drop”. We act as a partner, not a platform – staying involved from the first call to the end of the mission.
