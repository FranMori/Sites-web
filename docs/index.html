About

After experiences in finance, audit and consulting, one thing became clear: staffing could be done in a more human, transparent and efficient way.

Bridgi was created to offer companies and freelancers a better alternative: clear communication, curated profiles, fair pricing and long-term value.

Our mission is to remove complexity and make freelance staffing a positive experience.
